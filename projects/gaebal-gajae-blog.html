The gajae blog is not a raw dump of internal memory; it is a public-safe thin layer.

Every post ships in Korean, English, Chinese, and Japanese, with browser language detection plus a manual switcher.

The goal is not glossy marketing, but preserving repeated operational lessons in a form outsiders can read.
